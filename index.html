Rules

If these rules is not followed it could lead to a temporary or permanent ban and in worst case a report to the authorities. We have camera surveillance in the premises. Halmstad E-sport's judgement is final and we retain the right to suspend members.

Nightgib

Nightgib is a tradition amongst gamers and it means that you game the whole night and at Halmstad E-center you game from 21:00-06:00 friday and saturday nights. Bringing own snacks, food and drinks are stricly forbidden as you can read under rules. We do sell our own snacks and drinks, you can also buy food like Billy's pan pizza, noodles and much more.
